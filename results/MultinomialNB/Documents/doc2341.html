----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT SUVA APPELLATE JURISDICTION CRIMINAL APPEAL NO: HAA0036 OF 2003S Between: MITIELI KALOUDIGIBECI Applicant And: THE STATE Respondent Counsel: Mr. A.K. Singh for Applicant Mr. S. Leweniqila for State Hearing: 9th October 2003 Ruling: 9th October 2003 RULING The State does not oppose bail in the circumstances. I grant bail in the sum of $500 in his own recognizance with one surety in the same amount. He must report to the Nausori Police Station twice a week on Wednesdays and Saturdays between 6am and 6pm. He must attend court when he receives a notice of hearing from the Magistrates Court. Nazhat Shameem JUDGE At Suva 9th October 2003
