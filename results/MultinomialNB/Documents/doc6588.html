----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION CRIMINAL CASE NO: HAC 242/2011 BETWEEN: THE STATE AND: ISEI KORODRAU COUNSEL: Ms A Vavadakua for the State Mr R Vananalagi for the Accused Dates of Trial: 28-31/10/2013 Date of Summing Up: 01/11/2013 Date of Judgment: 01/11/2013 [Name of the victim is suppressed. She will be referred to as AKB] JUDGMENT 1. The three assessors have expressed unanimous opinion that the accused is guilty of the charge of Rape. The unanimous opinion is completely tenable on the basis of evidence considered in accordance with the legal directions that have been made. 2. I have considered and evaluated the evidence, applying legal principles contained in my summing-up to the assessors. 3. It is also my decision that accused is guilty of the charge of Rape. 4. The judgment of this court is that that the accused is guilty of Rape of AKB. He is convicted accordingly. P Kumararatnam JUDGE At Suva 01/11/2013
