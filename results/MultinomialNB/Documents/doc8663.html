----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION Criminal Case No: HAC 134 of 2008 STATE V PAULIASI VATUNALABA Hearing: 11th & 29th March 2010 Judgment: 29th March 2010 Counsel: Ms W. George for State Mr. T. Terere for Accused JUDGMENT [1] The Accused is charged with an offence of larceny by servant. The assessors have expressed unanimous opinions that he is guilty of the charge. [2] I remind myself in terms of the directions I gave the assessors and I have considered the evidence presented at the trial. [3] On the evidence adduced, I am satisfied beyond a reasonable doubt that the Accused stole copper wires from his employer Digicel Fiji Limited. I am satisfied beyond a reasonable doubt that the Accused had an intention to permanently deprive his employer when he took the copper wires from his employers warehouse, sometime in the month of June or July of 2008. [4] I accept the opinions of the assessors and find the Accused guilty of larceny by servant. Accordingly, the Accused is convicted as charged. Daniel Goundar JUDGE At Suva 29th March 2010 Solicitors: Office of the DPP for State Office of the Legal Aid Commission for Accused
