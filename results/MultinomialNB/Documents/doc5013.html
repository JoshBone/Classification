AT SUVA APPELLATE JURISDICTION CRIMINAL APPEAL NO: HAA 096 OF 2001S (Nausori MC Case No. 50 of 2001) Between: SAVENACA SALILI Appellant And: THE STATE Respondent Hearing: 26th April 2002 Judgment: 26th April 2002 Counsel: Appellant in Person Mr M. Korovou for Respondent JUDGMENT The appeal against conviction is dismissed. There is no ambiguity in the plea and the Appellant agrees that he understood the plea and the facts. The appeal against sentence is dismissed. The Magistrate gave him the minimum sentence possible under the Dangerous Drugs Act. Nazhat Shameem JUDGE At Suva 26th April 2002
