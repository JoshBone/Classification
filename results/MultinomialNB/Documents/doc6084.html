AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION Criminal Case No: HAC 40 of 2011 BETWEEN: STATE AND: SURESH CHANDRA BEFORE : HON. MR. JUSTICE PAUL K. MADIGAN Counsel : Ms T. Leweni for State Mr. I. Khan with Mr. M. Degei for Accused Dates of Hearing : 2nd, 3rd, 4th October 2012 Date of Summing Up : 5th October 2012 Date of Judgment : 5th October 2012 JUDGMENT [1] Suresh Chandra, you have been charged with the following offence: Statement of Offence MURDER: Contrary to section 237 of the Crimes Decree No. 44 of 2009. Particulars of Offence SURESH CHANDRA on the 15th day of January, 2011 at Nasinu in the Central Division murdered Farzana Begum. [2] In the majority opinion of three assessors you have been found guilty of the charge. The case as prosecuted was a largely circumstantial case, and as such the circumstantial evidence against you was strong. [3] I agree with the majority verdict of the assessors and find you guilty as charged. You are convicted accordingly. That is the judgment of the Court. Paul K. Madigan JUDGE At Suva 5th October 2012
