----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION CRIMINAL CASE NO: HAC 99/2010 BETWEEN: THE STATE AND: EPARAMA MANI COUNSEL: Mr L Fotofili for the State Accused in person Date of Trial: 18-22/03/2013 Date of Summing-Up: 25/03/2013 Judgment: 26/03/2013 JUDGMENT 1. The assessors have expressed unanimous opinions that the accused is guilty of Committing Aggravated Robbery and Grievous Harm. The unanimous opinion is completely tenable on the basis of evidence considered in accordance with the legal directions that have been made. 2. I have considered and evaluated the evidence, applying legal principles contained in my summing-up to the assessors. 3. It is also my decision that accused guilty of Committing Aggravated Robbery and Grievous Harm. 4. The judgment of this court is that that the accused is guilty of Committing Aggravated Robbery and Grievous Harm. P Kumararatnam JUDGE At Suva 26/03/2013
