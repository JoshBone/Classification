----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION CRIMINAL CASE NO: HAC 028 OF 2003S STATE V RATU JOPE SENILOLI RATU RAKUITA VAKALALABURE RATU VILIAME VOLAVOLA ISIRELI LEWENIQILA PECELI RINAKAMA VILIAME SAVU Counsel: Mr. M. Tedeschi, Mr. G. Allan & Ms A. Prasad for State; Mr. M. Raza for 1st Accused; Mr. A.K. Singh for 2nd Accused; Mr. S. Naqase for 3rd & 6th Accused; Mr. D. Sharma for 4th Accused; Mr. A. Seru for 5th Accused. Hearing: 28th June 2004 Judgment: 5th August 2004 JUDGMENT The majority opinion of the assessors is that the 1st, the 2nd, the 3rd, the 5th and the 6th accused are guilty of the offence with which they have been charged. I direct myself in accordance with my summing up. I agree with the opinions of the assessors and I convict the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 5th and 6th accused accordingly. The unanimous verdict of the assessors is that the 4th accused is not guilty of the charge. Again I direct myself in accordance with my summing up and I acquit him. Nazhat Shameem JUDGE At Suva 5th August 2004
