----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT LABASA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION Criminal Case No: HAC0003 of 2004 STATE v. MOHAMMED SAIYAAZ f/n Mohammed Usman MOHAMMED SHANEEN HUSSEIN f/n Mohammed Usman Hearing: 26th May 2005 Judgment: 1st June 2005 Counsel: Mr. P. Bulamainaivalu for State Mr. A.
