----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION Crim. Case No: HAC0022 of 2003S STATE v. FORAETE EPINISI JIONE Hearing: 18th October 31st October 2005 Judgment: 31st October 2005 Counsel: Mr. A. Ravindra-Singh for State Accused in Person JUDGMENT All three Assessors have given their opinions that the Accused is guilty on each count as charged. I have directed myself in accordance with my summing up. I agree with the Assessors and I find the Accused guilty on each count of the Information as charged. I convict him accordingly. Nazhat Shameem JUDGE At Suva 31st October 2005
