----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT LAUTOKA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. HBM0020 OF 2003L BETWEEN: KAMALNAYAN HARIBHAI PATEL Appellant AND: THE STATE Respondent Counsel for the Appellant: Mr. Iqbal Khan Counsel for the State : Mr. Kevueli Tunidau Date of Hearing & Judgment: 24 February 2004 JUDGMENT The prosecutor having consented to the appeal, I make the following Orders 1. That the convictions be set aside; 2. That the matters are remitted to the Magistrates Court for retrial before another magistrate; 3. That bail be extended to 29th of March 2004 when the accused is to appear before the Magistrates Court Lautoka. JOHN CONNORS JUDGE AT LAUTOKA 24 FEBRUARY 2004
