----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT LABASA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION CRIMINAL CASE NO. HAC 33 of 2008 BETWEEN: THE STATE AND: ABDUL AIYAZ Counsel: Mr. T Muloilagi & Mr. M Kaisamy for the State Mr. A Kholi for the Accused Date of Judgment: Wednesday 2nd September, 2009 JUDGMENT One assessor has found the accused guilty while two assessors have found the accused not guilty on a charge of found in possession of an illicit drug. The majority opinions of not guilty are available on the evidence. I direct myself in accordance with my summing up and accept the majority opinions of not guilty. I find the accused not guilty and acquit him of the charge. Daniel Goundar JUDGE At Labasa Wednesday 2nd September, 2009
