----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT LABASA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION CRIMINAL CASE NO. HAC 009 OF 2010 BETWEEN: STATE V. DEWAKAR DAYAL Counsel: Ms. M. Fong for the State Mr. A. Sen with Mr. K. Padayachi for the for Accused Date of Hearing: 22nd to 26th August 2011 Date of Summing Up: 29th August 2011 Date of Judgment: 29th August 2011 JUDGMENT [1] Dewakar Dayal, you have been charged with the following offence: Statement of Offence MURDER: Contrary to Section 273 of the Crimes Decree No. 44 of 2009. Particulars of Offence Dewakar Dayal s/o Ram Dayal, between the 12th day of March 2010 and the 13th day of March 2010 at Dreketi in the Northern Division murdered Ronita Devi d/o Girwar Prasad. [2] In the unanimous opinion of three assessors you have been found not guilty of the offence, and in directing myself on my own summing up and then being opinions rendered on a purely factual basis, I will not interfere. I find you not guilty of the offence; you are acquitted and discharged. Paul K. Madigan Judge At Labasa 29th August 2011
