----------------- IN THE HIGH COURT OF FIJI AT SUVA CRIMINAL JURISDICTION Crim. Case No: HAC0004 of 2005S STATE v. JOSUA NATAKURU Hearing: 4th 15th October 2005 Judgment: 15th October 2005 Counsel: Mr. D. Prasad & Ms S. Puamau for State Accused in Person JUDGMENT Both assessors agree that the Accused is guilty of rape as charged. I have directed myself in accordance with my summing up. I accept the evidence of Vasemaca Radinivanua and I agree with the assessors that the Accused is guilty. I convict him accordingly. Nazhat Shameem JUDGE At Suva 15th October 2005
