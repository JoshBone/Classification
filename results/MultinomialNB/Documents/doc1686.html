AT SUVA FIJI PUBLIC SERVICE ASSOCIATION v PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION & ANOR Civil Appeal CBV0002.09 Supreme Court, Fiji 24th March 2009 Gates, President ORDERS Mr V. Kapadia and Ms R. Naidu for the Petitioner Mr C. Pryde [Solicitor General], Mr S. Sharma and Ms M. Rakuita for the 1st and 2nd Respondents [1] For completeness sake, I will grant a stay on implementation of the Court of Appeal decision till the hearing of the application for stay on 31st March 2009 or any adjourned hearing thereof. This is subject to the decision of the single judge when the matter comes before him however. [2] I make this decision without considering the normal criteria for stay, and simply to get the parties to the stay application hearing. A.H.C.T.
